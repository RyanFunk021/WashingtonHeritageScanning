Common Questions

Do I ever have to ship or mail my photos?

Never. Every photo is dropped off and picked up in person within Washington, Illinois, or collected via our complimentary porch pickup service (Heritage Legacy Box tier). Your originals never touch a shipping carrier.

What if there's writing on the back of a photo?

Our automatic double-sided scanning captures the back of every photo by default, at no extra charge, so names, dates, and handwritten notes are preserved alongside the image.

How do I receive my finished digital photos?

You'll get a secure private Google Drive link for instant cloud access, so you and your family can view and download your photos right away.
